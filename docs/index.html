Mixup
A notebook and calculator
for pottery glazes.

Glaze Chemistry Made Easier

Mixup is a Mac program that keeps track of your glazes and materials. It also helps you understand your glazes better by breaking them down into the oxides that make up all of the materials potters use.

By looking at glazes in terms of oxides you can:

  • Understand why a glaze might be crazing
  • Formulate a similar glaze with new materials
  • Create new glazes from scratch
