Mixup
A notebook and calculator
for pottery glazes.

Mixup is written by Ken Chin-Purcell. I'm a potter and a programmer.

There a many programs for managing glazes, and I encourage you to check them out and pick the best one for your needs. They include:

  • Glazy
  • HyperGlaze
  • Matrix
  • Insight

I point these out because Mixup takes a simplified and fairly opinionated approach, so it may not be what you're looking for. I wrote it to scratch an itch, it's the program I wanted to keep track of my glazes and create new ones. Maybe it will be your program too.
